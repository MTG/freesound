Freesound categorizes sounds into broad categories using the Broad Sound Taxonomy (BST). The BST is designed to be a simple yet comprehensive system to organize sounds. By using this taxonomy, we ensure that sounds are easier to find, whether you're searching, browsing, or filtering results. The BST was added to Freesound on April 2025.
